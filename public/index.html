International Patient Summary demo

This service allows you to obtain your International Patient Summary as a digital credential in your wallet.

You can either

  • test the service using the pre-filled IPS bundle, or
  • replace the content below with your an IPS bundle acquired elsewhere, or
  • log in to Epic on FHIR sandbox to fetch your IPS data.
