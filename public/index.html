International Patient Summary demo

This service allows you to obtain your International Patient Summary (IPS) as a digital credential in your wallet.

You can either

  • test the service using the pre-filled IPS bundle, or
  • replace the content below with your an IPS bundle acquired elsewhere, or
  • use the button below to log in to an Epic sandbox patient portal to fetch your IPS data.
